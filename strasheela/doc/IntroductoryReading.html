Introductory Reading

This tutorial can not really introduce the Oz programming language itself. Oz is already very well documented. Still, the massive amount of documentation may discourage a musician mainly interested in Strasheela itself. Therefore, I recommend to read at least the following texts to get started with Strasheela.

  • The Tutorial of Oz explains the basics. I recommend to read at least chapter 1-7 plus 10
  • Finite Domain Constraint Programming in Oz. A Tutorial introduces how constraint programming is done in Oz. In particular, consider chapter 1-3, 5, and 8.
  • The thesis Composing Music by Composing Rules: Design and Usage of a Generic Music Constraint System explains the design of Strasheela in detail. I particularly recommend reading the chapters 4-7.
